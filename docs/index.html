ISIC 4922 • Intercity Coach / Chartered Bus Dispatch

A complete, governed, open-source actor system for independent intercity and chartered-coach operators to coordinate trip/ridership/incident-report logging, vehicle/route/timetable dispatch scheduling, fleet maintenance procurement with verified providers, and vehicle-defect/driver-fitness/route-hazard safety-concern flagging — all under independent verification and an immutable audit ledger.

Built on langgraph StateGraph runtime and the Intercity Coach Governor: an independent verification layer that re-derives every vehicle/route/operator-license and maintenance-provider's registration/verification status from the store itself — never from a proposal's own claim — before any dispatch commits, and permanently excludes directly finalizing a dispatch-safety-clearance or driver-fitness-to-drive determination.

Licensed AGPL-3.0-or-later. Designed to be forked, deployed, and operated by any qualified intercity/charter coach operator — for any route, in any country.

What this actor covers

  • Trip/ridership/incident-report data logging
  • Vehicle/route/timetable dispatch scheduling, gated on an independently verified vehicle/route/operator-license record
  • Fleet maintenance procurement coordination, gated on an independently verified maintenance provider, with mandatory human sign-off above a cost threshold
  • Vehicle-defect/driver-fitness/route-hazard concern flagging — always routed to a human, never self-resolved
  • Immutable audit ledger for every log, schedule, order and flag decision
  • Directly finalizing a dispatch-safety-clearance or driver-fitness-to-drive determination is permanently out of scope, not a rollout milestone
